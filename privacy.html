隱私權政策

最後更新：2026-06-20 · 發行者 SigNote · 聯絡 signote.app@gmail.com

繁中在前，English below.

1. 在裝置上、不外傳的部分

SigNote（以下稱「本 App」）的核心功能——選照片、相機拍攝、去背線稿、塗色、加前景與濾鏡、存檔——全部在你的裝置本機完成。你的相機畫面與相簿照片不會被上傳，除非你主動執行「分享」或「上傳到社群」。

2. 帳號（貼紙社群功能）

當你使用「SigNote 廣場」並登入時，我們透過你選擇的 Google 或 Sign in with Apple 取得一組使用者識別碼；視登入方式與你的選擇，亦可能包含你的電子郵件或顯示名稱。我們不會取得你的密碼。你可隨時於該帳號設定中撤銷授權。用途：辨識你的上傳與按讚、維護帳號。

3. 你上傳的內容（UGC）

若你選擇把塗色貼紙「上傳到社群」，該貼紙圖片與你填寫的名稱會儲存到我們的雲端服務，並公開給其他使用者瀏覽、下載與按讚。請勿上傳含個人隱私、他人著作權或不當內容的圖片。你可要求刪除你上傳的內容（見第 7 點）。

4. 按讚與互動

你對貼紙的按讚會被記錄，用以顯示讚數與你的按讚狀態。

5. 我們不做的事

本 App 不含廣告、不做使用者追蹤（跨 App／網站）、不接分析 SDK、不為廣告目的出售或分享你的資料。

6. 雲端服務商（資料處理者）

社群功能的帳號、內容與圖檔儲存於 Supabase（後端即服務）。其伺服器在處理請求時會產生標準技術紀錄（如 IP 位址、時間戳）。Supabase 僅作為我們的資料處理者。

7. 你的權利

你可要求查詢、更正或刪除你的帳號與上傳內容。請來信 signote.app@gmail.com，我們會在合理時間內處理。你也可在 App 內刪除自己的帳號（貼紙社群 → 設定）。

8. 兒童

本 App 為一般客群，非專為兒童設計。社群為使用者產生內容，我們提供檢舉與下架機制以維護內容品質。

9. 變更

本政策如有更新將於此頁公告並更新日期。

10. 聯絡

有任何隱私問題請來信 signote.app@gmail.com。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20 · Developer: SigNote

1. On-device, never uploaded

The App's core features — choosing photos, camera capture, line-art cutout, coloring, foregrounds and filters, saving — all run locally on your device. Your camera feed and library photos are not uploaded unless you explicitly tap Share or Upload to Community.

2. Account (Community feature)

When you sign in to use the Sticker Community, we obtain a user identifier via your chosen provider — Google or Sign in with Apple — and possibly your email or display name depending on the method and your choice. We never receive your password. You can revoke access anytime in that account's settings. Purpose: to attribute your uploads and likes and maintain your account.

3. Your uploaded content (UGC)

If you upload a coloring sticker to the Community, that image and the title you enter are stored in our cloud service and made public to other users to browse, download, and like. Do not upload images containing private information, others' copyrighted material, or inappropriate content. You may request deletion of your content (see §7).

4. Likes

Your likes are recorded to show like counts and your like state.

5. What we don't do

The App contains no ads, no cross-app/website tracking, no analytics SDKs, and we do not sell or share your data for advertising.

6. Processor

Community accounts, content, and images are stored on Supabase (backend-as-a-service), which generates standard technical logs (e.g., IP, timestamps) while serving requests, acting solely as our processor.

7. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account and uploaded content by emailing signote.app@gmail.com. You can also delete your account in-app (Community → Settings).

8. Children

The App is general-audience. The Community is user-generated; we provide reporting and takedown to maintain quality.

9. Changes

Updates will be posted here with a revised date.

10. Contact
