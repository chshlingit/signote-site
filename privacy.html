隱私權政策

最後更新：2026-06-21 · 發行者 SigNote · 聯絡 signote.app@gmail.com

繁中在前，English below.

1. 全部在你的裝置上完成

SigNote（以下稱「本 App」）的所有功能——選照片、相機拍攝、去背線稿、塗色、加前景與濾鏡、存檔——都在你的裝置本機完成。你的相機畫面與相簿照片不會被上傳到我們的伺服器。

2. 我們不蒐集個人資料

本 App 不需要註冊或登入、不建立帳號，也不蒐集你的姓名、電子郵件或位置等個人資料，更不會把你的照片或作品傳到我們這裡。

3. 分享與輸出

當你主動點「分享」或「輸出」時，圖片是經由 iOS 系統分享表單交給你選擇的目的地（相簿、訊息、其他 App）。該行為與後續資料處理，依你所選 App／服務本身的政策規範，與本 App 無關。

4. 相機與相簿權限

相機與相簿權限僅用於讓你選取或拍攝要編輯的照片；處理結果留在你的裝置上，由你決定是否存檔或分享。

5. 我們不做的事

本 App 不含廣告、不做使用者追蹤（跨 App／網站）、不接分析 SDK、不為廣告目的出售或分享你的資料。

6. 兒童

本 App 為一般客群，非專為兒童設計。

7. 變更

本政策如有更新將於此頁公告並更新日期。

8. 聯絡

有任何隱私問題請來信 signote.app@gmail.com。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-21 · Developer: SigNote

1. Everything happens on your device

All of the App's features — choosing photos, camera capture, line-art cutout, coloring, foregrounds and filters, saving — run locally on your device. Your camera feed and library photos are not uploaded to our servers.

2. We don't collect personal data

The App requires no registration or sign-in and creates no account. We do not collect your name, email, or location, and we never send your photos or creations to us.

3. Sharing & exporting

When you tap Share or Export, the image is handed to your chosen destination (Photos, Messages, another app) via the iOS share sheet. What happens next is governed by that app/service's own policy, not ours.

4. Camera & photo permissions

Camera and photo-library access is used only so you can pick or capture a photo to edit; the result stays on your device, and you decide whether to save or share it.

5. What we don't do

The App contains no ads, no cross-app/website tracking, no analytics SDKs, and we do not sell or share your data for advertising.

6. Children

The App is general-audience and not specifically directed at children.

7. Changes

Updates will be posted here with a revised date.
